Travel Tips

For our guests travelling from Finland 🇫🇮

✈️
Getting There
Direct flights from Helsinki (HEL) to Vilnius (VNO) ~1.5h. Airlines: Finnair, Ryanair.
🏨
Accommodation
Recommended: Stikliai Hotel, Artagonist Hotel, or Airbnb in Vilnius Old Town.
🚗
Getting Around
Bolt & Uber work great in Vilnius. Car rental also available at the airport.
💶
Currency
Lithuania uses Euro (€). Cards accepted almost everywhere. ATMs widely available.
